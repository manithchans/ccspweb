CCSP Distance Learning Program

Learn English with CCSP is a new program, created by CCSP for everyone especially children to learn English and for those who want to expand the knowledge of the English Language. Vocabularies will be excerpted from Grade 6 English textbook published by the Ministry of Education and Youth. During this virus outbreak, we want to contribute to the education system in Cambodia by introducing this E-learning program thus parents or guardian can encourage children to learn English at home. Finally, we hope everyone would support and enjoy this program and we will see you soon. Thank you.
