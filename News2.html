Covid-19 Prevention Workshop

Last week, CCSP hosted a workshop with our students at Phnom Penh Thmey Primary School. which was under a hot topic about the new outbreak of Coronavirus. It was such a meaningful and educational workshop where everyone including students and teachers got to learn about the coronavirus, what, where and why is it so threatening to people. At the beginning and end of the session, we let them clean their hand with hand sanitizer and distributing masks for protection. Stay clean by washing your hand regularly is the priority prevention for everyone from Coronavirus. Be safe and healthy!
