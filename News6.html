CCSP 2020 Mandate Orientation Day

Committee for Community Service Program (CCSP) had hosted an orientation day for our most potential members who are currently the students at the American University of Phnom Penh. We provided meaningful training to our new members to help them prepare with their English teaching as a second language to young kids at Phnom Penh Thmey Primary School. During the orientation day, there were many activities, such as introducing the volunteers to CCSP, ice-breaking game, conducting sharing session with the former volunteers. We provided the volunteering teacher this supportive training and clear lesson plans in order to make them feel comfortable, confident, and be ready to become a teacher in the upcoming academic year of 2019 - 2020.

Being a part of CCSP not only builds experiences in volunteering, but it also brings about connection, communication, and creates joys with all of the members and especially the children. Moreover, each member will also get to learn two essential skills - leadership and teamworking.

We believe that the contribution of time and energy from our most remarkable members would not only allow them to experience new things, but also give them the opportunity to construct the brightest road to every Cambodian children who deserve a better education.

"Together, we make things better" - CCSP
