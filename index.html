OUR MISSION

Initiated by students at American University of Phnom Penh (AUPP), Committee for Community Service Program (CCSP) is a student-led program, which aims to make positive change in local community through youth volunteerism and community engagement.

Youth Volunteerism

In 2016, CCSP started off by establishing an English Teaching Department. We seeks to provide hands-on experiences for youths to volunteer as English teachers.

Community Engagement

We also intends to fill gaps amidst a shortage of English teachers at local primary schools. We has successfully conducted two English teaching programs at two public primary schools.

Spread Health Awareness

CCSP aims to establish its newly-formed Health Workshop Department, which possesses the mission to raise awareness on various health-related topics to our students.
