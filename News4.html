University of Arizona students visiting CCSP

CCSP was very pleased to welcome students and staff from the University of Arizona to visit our community on Saturday.
UA students were very delighted to see many intelligent and enthusiastic children. Throughout the day, our community and UA students have shared so much love, kindness, and more than that exchanging the culture between Cambodia and America.
Everyone had such a great time together with a lot of fun and joyful activities. We hope to see more interactions between our CCSP community and the University of Arizona in the near future.
